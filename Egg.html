Fried Egg

A fried egg is the start of a good morning. Pair with toast and bacon for a treat.

Steps:

  1. Place small slice of butter in pan, heat on (7).
  2. Spread butter
  3. Once butter melted, crack egg into pan
  4. Cook first side ~ 3min then flip. Egg should be mostly solid.
  5. Heat off, let sit 1min.
  6. Enjoy.
